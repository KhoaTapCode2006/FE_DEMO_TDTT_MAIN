About the Property

Experience the epitome of French colonial architecture blended with traditional Vietnamese elegance. The Grand Heritage Resort & Spa offers a tranquil oasis in the heart of Hanoi's bustling historic district. Each suite is meticulously curated with hand-woven silk tapestries and dark teak furnishings, offering an editorial retreat for the discerning traveler.
